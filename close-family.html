Shuang Zhang

Shuang was born in Urumqi, Xinjiang, in 1969. She grew up with a family of five (3 sisters, mother, father), and stayed there for ~16 years. She stayed in Urumqi for high school, and then went for university
in Hangzhou, Zhejiang. She visited
India in 1999 to be together with
Yuliang who went there for his PHD in 1998.

Then

Shuang made a lot of traditional foods in Urumqi with the help of her mother. She loves making chinese dishes including baozi (steamed buns), dumplings, and many more.

Now

Even though we have lived on Canada for over 15 total years, we eat traditional chinese food. We eat dumplings, rice, steamed buns, all of them. As I grow up, I will learn to cook these dishes which I can pass on through generations.
